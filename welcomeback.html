THE SAFETY AND WELL-BEING:

As the COVID-19 (coronavirus) situation continues to rapidly evolve, we know it is increasingly top of mind for our employees, and customers. Our number ONE priority right now and always is the health and safety of our employees, and customers.

We will continue to follow updates and protocol suggested by the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), local Departments of Public Health, and World Health Organization (WHO) to best ensure the safety of our customers and team members.

Kalpasi is dedicated to the cleanliness of our restaurant, We have always prided ourselves on consistently maintaining the highest levels of cleanliness and complying with the state department of health sanitation standards at our location.

Furthermore, here are some things you should expect in our restaurants:

  1. Strict, regimented cleaning, sanitizing and disinfecting procedures and schedules

  2. Team members wearing face coverings to protect themselves and others

  3. Dining rooms and bars with reduced seating to ensure proper social distancing

  4. Convenient and visible hand sanitizer bottles or stations available for use upon entrance

  5. Sanitized menus, and access to online menu on your own device

  6. Condiments available upon request and their containers disinfected after each use

  7. Ongoing adherence to strict team member health protocol requiring team members who do not feel well to stay home and seek proper health care, as needed

  8. Reduced number of guests in the waiting area to again adhere to social distancing

  9. A limited menu as our restaurants begin to reopen to ensure weâ€™re able to provide the best experience to each guest

Thank you for your support and patience as we get through this pandemic together.
