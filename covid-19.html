KALPASI IS OPEN FOR BUSINESS

Offers Curbside Pick-up & Door Delivery, (Contact-less Delivery)!

During these uncertain times, Kalpasi Indian Cuisine is here for you to support you as you hunker down and care for your family. We offer the convenience of having Freshly prepared, Authentic Indian food, family-style dishes as well as a kids’ menu and delivered at Curbside, or directly to your home, business or hotel.

HOW IT WORKS

  1. Make your selections from our menu and call Nathan with your orders at 703-429-4388 and your pickup/delivery time, and with your payment information.

  2. Drive to Kalpasi: your food will be ready at your scheduled pickup time.

  3. Pull up at the restaurant parking lot, and we’ll place your order in your car.
